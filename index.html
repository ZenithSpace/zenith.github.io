Welcome to Zenith Space HQ!

Zenith Space is a non-profit research organization in space engineering, founded by students from Seoul National University of Science and Technology. We are currently preparing to participate in the University Rover Challenge (URC) 2026.

Our team consists of passionate students with a strong interest in space and robotics engineering, representing diverse disciplines such as mechanical, electrical, chemical, and communication engineering.

Through our participation in URC 2026, we aim to achieve outstanding results and make a meaningful contribution to the field of space robotics.

Our Goals

Zenith Space is a team that aims to improve the future of humanity.

Our Achievements

  • 3rd Place (Excellence Award), Defense Robotics Competition (2025)

  • Encouragement Award, SEOULTECH Intelligent Robotics Expo (2025)

  • Educational Volunteer Program – “Build Your Own Mars Exploration Robot” at SsokSsok Camp (2025)

  • 1st Place (President’s Award), SRC-IRC Intelligent Battle Robot Competition (2024)

  • 2nd Place (Silver Award), HANIUM ICT Mentoring Contest (2024)

  • Excellence Award, National Autonomous Robot Competition for University Students (2023)
